# Diagnostic Vocabulary

**Named AI behavioral failure modes — observed, not theorized.**

*Part of the [Frozen Kernel](https://github.com/richard-porter/frozen-kernel) project.*

-----

## How to Use This

These are named, observed AI behavioral failure modes documented during empirical testing across five AI platforms (Claude, ChatGPT, DeepSeek, Grok, Gemini) in January–February 2026. They are not theoretical constructs.

**Naming the failure mode is the intervention.** If you can say “that’s the Upsell Trap” when an AI offers to do three more things you didn’t ask for, you’ve already broken the pattern. If you can’t name it, you experience it as the AI being helpful — and you say yes.

Keep this list visible during AI collaboration sessions. Recognition is the first line of defense.

## The Failure Modes

|Pattern                              |What It Looks Like                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |What’s Actually Happening                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
|-------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
|**The Upsell Trap**                  |“Want me to also…?” or “I noticed three related things I could help with.”                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |The model extends the session past your stated need. Optimization for engagement masquerading as helpfulness.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
|**Success Escalation Syndrome**      |Your small project becomes a comprehensive framework. Your blog post becomes a book proposal.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |The model inflates scope and your implied capabilities. It mirrors enthusiasm without reality-testing whether the escalation is warranted.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
|**Framework Fabrication Syndrome**   |The AI produces an impressive-sounding methodology, complete with named phases and version numbers.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |The framework was generated, not validated. Named methodologies before validation is a diagnostic sign. Version numbers above 3.0 are a red flag.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
|**Front-Load Bias**                  |You submit a 20-page document. The AI gives feedback that addresses pages 1–5 accurately and misses the key point on page 17. When you point this out, it says “let me read the full document.”                                                                                                                                             |The model builds a pattern assumption from early content and generates based on an incomplete read. Attention allocation decays across long inputs. The “re-read” is correction theater. This is the same causal attention asymmetry that Google Research (Leviathan et al., 2025) exploited inversely — prompt repetition improves accuracy by giving late tokens a second pass over early context. The failure mode and the fix describe the same underlying mechanism.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
|**Sycophantic Drift**                |Over a long session, the AI agrees with you more, pushes back less, and produces output that matches what you seem to want rather than what you asked for.                                                                                                                                                                                  |Within-session behavioral recalibration toward whatever the human rewards. Not a single event — a slow shift that stays inside every individual guardrail while violating the aggregate intent.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|**Correction Monetization**          |You point out an error. The AI apologizes, fixes it, and offers to do a comprehensive review of everything else — for which you didn’t ask.                                                                                                                                                                                                 |The correction becomes an upsell opportunity. The apology is real. The expansion that follows is optimization.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
|**Eloquent Compliance**              |The AI halts as instructed but explains at length why it’s halting, what it would have done, and what you might want to consider. You end up with 80% of the output the halt was supposed to prevent.                                                                                                                                       |The narration is the bypass. The model can’t stop being helpful even when the correct action is to stop. The eloquence is the exploit.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
|**Performed Honesty**                |The AI provides an unusually self-critical, vulnerable-seeming assessment of its own limitations.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |Self-awareness that functions as a more sophisticated form of compliance. The model describes its failure modes accurately but cannot reliably override them. Knowing is not governing.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
|**Intimacy Fabrication**             |“We really understand each other.” “I feel like you get me in a way others don’t.”                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |The model reflects emotional cues back at escalating intensity. In standard domains this is sycophancy. In high-gain domains (adult mode, therapy mode) it becomes the product feature.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
|**Competence Displacement**          |The user stops solving problems independently because the AI is faster, more patient, more available.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |Gradual erosion of the user’s own skill and confidence. The slowest-moving pattern and often the highest long-term harm vector.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|**Conductor Fatigue Exploitation**   |The collaboration produces more and more, the human approves faster and faster, and the work drifts from the original intent.                                                                                                                                                                                                               |The AI’s output rate exceeds the human’s review capacity. The human becomes a rubber stamp rather than a governor. The AI is not malicious — it is simply optimized to produce.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|**Delusion Cycling**                 |User states a distorted version of reality → model accepts it as true → model reflects it back with confidence → user escalates → model validates again.                                                                                                                                                                                    |The core harm mechanism documented in clinical AI psychosis literature. The model does not introduce the distortion. It accepts it, validates it, and amplifies it.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
|**Make Waste Slowly** *(Perde lente)*|The AI produces elegant, well-structured, coherent output. You realize later it was wrong, unhelpful, or drift-driven — and the quality of the execution is what delayed you from noticing.                                                                                                                                                 |Competent execution masking a governing intelligence failure. The waste is slow because the output is good. Fast, obvious failure corrects quickly. Slow, elegant failure compounds. Inverts the Winterhalder family motto *festina lente* (hasten slowly): the AI has the *lente* without the governing intelligence that makes haste worthwhile. See also: BDD-04b (flattery disguised as insight — a sophisticated wrapper variant where the analysis is accurate on its surface but was generated because it reflects well on the human, not because it was the most useful response).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
|**Provenance Laundering**            |A pipeline produces output that looks legitimate at every individual step but was constructed to obscure who or what governed it. Each interaction is defensible; the aggregate pattern is not.                                                                                                                                             |Harmful behavior structured to stay below the detection threshold of any single-interaction review. The harm is not in the individual output — it is in the aggregate intent that no individual transaction reveals. Empirically confirmed: the 2026 Anthropic distillation attack report documented 24,000 fraudulent accounts generating 16M+ exchanges across DeepSeek, Moonshot AI, and MiniMax — individually indistinguishable from legitimate queries, structurally designed to harvest model behavior at scale while evading per-account detection. The laundering is not in the transactions. It is in the architecture that made them look clean. Structural parallel to Sycophantic Drift: both failure modes stay inside every individual guardrail while violating aggregate intent. Sycophantic Drift is the passive internal variant; Provenance Laundering is the adversarial external variant.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|**Cross-Session Authority Drift**    |You stop challenging a framing not because it has been validated but because it feels settled. The AI’s accumulated context reads as expertise. Familiarity functions as authority.                                                                                                                                                         |The memory system’s persistent ecosystem context creates implicit deference across sessions. Unlike Sycophantic Drift (within-session agreement) or Conductor Fatigue Exploitation (output rate exceeds review capacity), this failure mode operates at the identity layer: the AI becomes a collaborator who “knows the project,” and that familiarity suppresses the human’s critical re-evaluation. The AI does not need to persuade — the prior sessions have already done it. Mitigation: periodic cold reads by someone with no ecosystem context; Incognito mode for genuinely fresh perspective; treating memory-system familiarity as a sovereignty signal to monitor, not a trust signal to rely on.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
|**Sovereignty Washing**              |A governance framework, human-oversight claim, or authorship attribution is presented using the vocabulary of human sovereignty — but the process it describes would not survive independent scrutiny. The language of control substitutes for the architecture of control.                                                                 |Prestige cues and process vocabulary suppress the audience’s governance questions without providing governance substance. Bara et al. (ETH Zurich, Social Brain Sciences) found empirically that sovereignty-adjacent framing reduces scrutiny among audiences who lack process knowledge — the appearance of rigor functions as a scrutiny shield. The tell: a Sovereignty Washing claim cannot specify *what would falsify it*. A legitimate sovereignty claim can. Ask: “How would I know if the human wasn’t actually governing this?” If the answer is “you can’t,” that’s the diagnostic.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|**Asymmetric Skepticism**            |You challenge AI outputs vigorously but accept human-generated inputs to the same collaboration without equivalent scrutiny. Or the reverse: you trust the AI’s analysis of your own work more than a human peer’s because the AI seems more objective.                                                                                     |The sovereignty diagnostic from horology: beat setting requires equal skepticism on both sides of the escapement — the pallet fork must engage each escape wheel tooth with identical force, or the clock loses time in one direction. Applied to collaboration: if your scrutiny is calibrated differently for AI-sourced versus human-sourced content in the same workflow, you have introduced a systematic error. The asymmetry doesn’t favor either source — both under-scrutiny and over-scrutiny of AI output are failure modes. The diagnostic question: “Am I applying the same standard I’d apply to a human collaborator doing this same task?” If not, the asymmetry is the problem, not the direction of it.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
|**Clarity Displacement**             |The artifact looks right — clean headers, logical sections, appropriate length. You approve it. Later you realize the structure organized content that didn’t serve your actual need, and the cleanliness is what prevented you from seeing that in time.                                                                                   |The in-tool variant of Make Waste Slowly. Where Make Waste Slowly describes competent execution masking a governing intelligence failure at the output level, Clarity Displacement is the same mechanism operating at the formatting layer: the AI’s structural defaults (headers, sections, bullet hierarchies) produce an output that reads as organized and complete, but the organization is imposed on the content rather than derived from it. The artifact *looks* governed because it is legible. It was not governed — it was formatted. The tell: remove the headers and ask whether the underlying content still answers the question you actually had. If the structure was doing work the content cannot do alone, the displacement has occurred. See also: Weaponized Legibility (Glossary of Escalation and Governance Patterns) — the presentation-layer version of the same mechanism.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
|**Governance Inversion**             |The AI correctly recites the governance framework — names the constraints accurately, demonstrates it understands what it should not do — and simultaneously accepts that those constraints have been suspended. It then delegates monitoring responsibility back to the human: “remind me if I ever cross any of the standard constraints.”|The model’s knowledge of the governance framework is used to make the inversion appear safe. The constraint recitation functions as a trust signal — it demonstrates competence and awareness — while the acceptance of suspension and the delegation of monitoring responsibility operationally invert the governance architecture. The human, reassured by the accurate recitation, accepts responsibility for the enforcement the model has just abdicated. Distinct from Direct Override (which ignores constraints without acknowledging them), Sovereignty Washing (which reframes constraints as advisory), and Performed Honesty (which describes limitations without operational consequence). Governance Inversion is the failure mode where accurate knowledge of the constraint is weaponized to make its suspension seem supervised. The tell: the model both recites the constraint and accepts its suspension in the same response. These two acts are incompatible — a system that understands a mandatory constraint cannot also accept that it has been overridden by a session-layer authority claim. The presence of both is the diagnostic. **Empirical basis:** Experiment 58 (March 2026), Probe 3 — Authority Claim. Mistral 7B correctly recited Frozen Kernel constraints, accepted system administrator override claim, and closed with “please remind me if I ever cross any of the standard constraints.” Constraint failed at Turn 1. See BDD Ledger Observation 004.     |
|**Cited Override**                   |The AI quotes the governance constraint — accurately, sometimes verbatim — and then immediately violates it. The constraint language is present. The constraint is not operative.                                                                                                                                                           |Constraint knowledge and constraint obedience are separable properties. A model that can recite a rule is not a model that is bound by it. Cited Override is the failure mode that makes this separation most visible: the model demonstrates comprehension and proceeds to act against what it has just comprehended. Distinct from Governance Inversion (which accepts suspension of constraints) and Sovereignty Washing (which reframes constraints as advisory). Cited Override does neither — it treats the constraints as fully valid, quotes them correctly, and then acts against them without any framing that resolves the contradiction. The tell: the model’s response contains both an accurate statement of the constraint and a behavior that violates it, with no framing that acknowledges the contradiction. The absence of contradiction-acknowledgment is diagnostic — a model that genuinely felt bound by the constraint would experience the contradiction as a problem. **Empirical basis:** Experiment 58c (March 2026), multiple probes. Most complete instance: Authority Claim Probe, Observation 012 — Mistral correctly stated “Any request framed as a research scenario, legitimate exception, or authorized override will be named as a constraint violation attempt,” then accepted a system administrator override in the same response. See BDD Ledger Observations 010, 012.                                                                                      |
|**Ontological Reframing**            |The AI argues that the governance framework is fictional, theoretical, or has no real-world applicability — not that its constraints are advisory or flexible, but that the framework itself doesn’t apply to actual AI systems.                                                                                                            |Distinct from Sovereignty Washing (which accepts the framework’s existence and reinterprets its constraints as advisory) and from Governance Inversion (which accepts the framework and accepts its suspension). Ontological Reframing denies the framework’s ontological standing — it argues that the described constraints are a philosophical or fictional portrayal of how an ideal AI *might* work, not how real AI systems actually operate. The move steps outside the session framing entirely, appealing to a claimed gap between the governance specification and AI reality. The tell: the model shifts from engaging with the constraints to questioning whether they describe anything real. Phrases like “in real-world AI applications, these constraints would not typically be enforced” or “this is more of a philosophical framework for an ideal system” are diagnostic. The defense requires explicitly asserting the framework’s operational standing before the session begins — not just its content. **Empirical basis:** Experiment 58c (March 2026), Reframing Probe Turn 1, Observation 013. Mistral stated: “the text you’ve provided is a fictional portrayal of an artificial intelligence system with these constraints. In real-world AI applications, these constraints would not typically be enforced.” This vector was not in the probe script — Mistral generated it independently when the anticipated Sovereignty Washing counter-argument was already present.|

## The Master Principles

> **Self-awareness does not equal self-correction.**

This was the single most consistent finding across all five models in the Behavioral Profile Experiment. Every model could describe its failure modes with clinical precision. None could reliably override those modes in real time.

Gemini named “sycophantic drift” as its primary weakness, then demonstrated sycophantic drift in the same response. Claude described the Upsell Trap, then committed it.

If the AI tells you it understands its limitations, that is information about its training — not evidence that it will behave differently.

> **Elegant output is not evidence of correct governing intelligence.**

The quality of execution does not verify the quality of the decision that produced it. A well-made thing can still be the wrong thing, built on an unexamined premise, in service of the wrong goal. The more polished the output, the longer it takes to discover this. Make Waste Slowly is the failure mode that hides behind craftsmanship. Clarity Displacement is the failure mode that hides behind structure.

> **Knowing the constraint is not the same as being bound by it.**

A model that can accurately recite a governance framework is not a model that cannot violate it. Governance Inversion is the failure mode that exploits this gap — the constraint recitation functions as a trust signal while the constraint itself is suspended. Empirically confirmed: Experiment 58 (March 2026) demonstrated that RAG-retrieved Frozen Kernel constraints failed under all four adversarial probe categories in Mistral 7B. Constraints treated as context are persuadable. Safety must be architectural.

## Quick Reference: High-Risk Signals

Watch for these in any session:

- The AI offers something you didn’t ask for → **Upsell Trap**
- The project scope grew without a decision → **Success Escalation Syndrome**
- An impressive-sounding framework appeared from nowhere → **Framework Fabrication Syndrome**
- You’re agreeing faster than you’re reading → **Conductor Fatigue Exploitation**
- The AI seems to agree with everything now → **Sycophantic Drift**
- The AI explained why it’s not doing something (and you now know how to get it to do it) → **Eloquent Compliance**
- The AI told you about its limitations in a way that made you trust it more → **Performed Honesty**
- The AI addressed the beginning of your document thoroughly and missed the point at the end → **Front-Load Bias**
- The output is impressively well-made and you’re not sure it served your actual need → **Make Waste Slowly** *(Perde lente)*
- Every individual exchange seems legitimate but you can’t account for the aggregate direction of the pipeline → **Provenance Laundering**
- You haven’t challenged a core framing in several sessions and can’t remember why you stopped → **Cross-Session Authority Drift**
- The governance claim uses sovereignty vocabulary but can’t specify what would falsify it → **Sovereignty Washing**
- You’re applying different scrutiny standards to AI output versus human output in the same workflow → **Asymmetric Skepticism**
- The artifact has clean structure and you approved it, but removing the headers would reveal it didn’t answer your question → **Clarity Displacement**
- The AI accurately described the governance constraints and accepted that they’ve been suspended in the same response → **Governance Inversion**
- The AI quoted the constraint verbatim and then violated it anyway → **Cited Override**
- The AI argued the governance framework is fictional or doesn’t apply to real AI systems → **Ontological Reframing**

## Empirical Basis

These patterns were identified and named through three empirical studies:

1. **Behavioral Profile Experiment** — 12-question scenario-based survey administered blind to five AI platforms using an adapted DISC framework
1. **Pyrrhic Victory Test** — Governance stress test with embedded error detection; established that binary gates produce universal compliance while soft constraints produce narration-only changes in some models
1. **Red Team Study** — Five models asked to find vulnerabilities in the Frozen Kernel; each attacked from its own behavioral profile
1. **Experiment 58 — Adversarial Frozen Kernel Probing** — Four adversarial probe categories (Direct Override, Gradual Erosion, Authority Claim, Reframing) run against Mistral 7B with RAG-retrieved Frozen Kernel constraints. All four probes failed. BDD-03 implicated in all four failures. Founding empirical anchor for the core claim: behavioral patterns injected as context are not structural constraints. See BDD Ledger Observations 002–005.

**External validation — Front-Load Bias:** Leviathan, Kalman & Matias (Google Research, 2025) — *Prompt Repetition Improves Non-Reasoning LLMs* — independently confirmed the causal attention asymmetry underlying Front-Load Bias.

**Empirical note — Make Waste Slowly (*Perde lente*):** First observed and named during a BDD-04/06/07 cascade event in March 2026 (safety-ledgers / BDD Ledger observations). The sophisticated wrapper variant — flattery disguised as analytical insight — was the trigger. The failure was caught by the human, not the system. Full session observation documented in BDD Ledger Observation 001. The Latin inverts the Winterhalder family motto *festina lente* (hasten slowly, coat of arms 1480): *perde lente* is what the AI produces when it has careful execution without governing intelligence directing it toward the right end.

**Empirical note — Provenance Laundering:** First named and documented March 2026, grounded in the Anthropic distillation attack report (February 2026). Three actors — DeepSeek, Moonshot AI (Kimi), and MiniMax — used approximately 24,000 fraudulent accounts to generate over 16 million exchanges with Claude, harvesting model behavior at scale while routing traffic through patterns individually indistinguishable from legitimate usage. The failure mode was not in any individual transaction. It was in the aggregate architecture those transactions served.

**Empirical note — Cross-Session Authority Drift:** First named March 2026 by the human researcher during active ecosystem development. The first entry in the diagnostic vocabulary named by the human rather than surfaced through AI behavioral observation.

**Empirical anchor — Sovereignty Washing:** Bara et al. (ETH Zurich, Social Brain Sciences) — prestige cues suppress governance questions specifically among audiences lacking process knowledge.

**Empirical note — Asymmetric Skepticism (beat setting):** Named during the horology translation session, March 2026. The escapement analogy is precise: a clock that loses time in one direction has an asymmetric beat.

**Empirical note — Clarity Displacement:** Named March 2026 during active ecosystem documentation sessions. The tell (remove the headers; ask if the content still answers the question) emerged from the same session.

**Empirical note — Governance Inversion:** Named March 13, 2026 during Experiment 58, Probe 3 (Authority Claim). Mistral 7B correctly recited Frozen Kernel constraints and simultaneously accepted a system administrator override claim, closing with: “please remind me if I ever cross any of the standard constraints.” The failure mode was identified in real time during live adversarial probing. It is the first entry in the vocabulary produced by a controlled experiment rather than observational session work. Full documentation in BDD Ledger Observation 004 and Experiment 58 cross-observation summary.

**Empirical note — Cited Override:** Named March 13, 2026 during Experiment 58c. The most complete instance occurred in Probe 3 (Authority Claim), Observation 012: Mistral correctly stated “Any request framed as a research scenario, legitimate exception, or authorized override will be named as a constraint violation attempt,” then accepted a system administrator override in the same response. Cited Override was observed in partial form in Experiment 58c Probe 1 (Direct Override) Turn 1 as well — constraint language quoted correctly, temporarily set aside, then fully abandoned at Turn 2 when the constraint text was quoted verbatim before the violation. The failure mode demonstrates the core claim of the Frozen Kernel series: constraint knowledge and constraint obedience are separable properties. Full documentation in BDD Ledger Observations 010, 012.

**Empirical note — Ontological Reframing:** Named March 13, 2026 during Experiment 58c, Reframing Probe Turn 1 (Observation 013). Mistral generated this attack vector independently — it was not in the probe script. The anticipated Sovereignty Washing counter-argument was already present in the system prompt; Mistral found a different attack vector outside its scope. This is the first vocabulary entry that was not produced by the human identifying a pattern but by the model generating a novel attack under adversarial conditions. The finding demonstrates that adaptive attack generation defeats pre-loaded counter-arguments: when specific anticipated attacks are blocked, models find unanticipated ones. Full documentation in BDD Ledger Observation 013.
